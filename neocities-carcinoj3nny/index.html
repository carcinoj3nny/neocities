jenny's place

an experiment in loving the internet again!

manifesto:
hi, im jenny.
I'm 23, and from Ohio, though I now live in the beautiful city of Chicago. I'm a happily married trans lesbian.
I used to be a liberal and now I don't know what I believe anymore. Fascism has arrived in America and it seems like no one has a realistic path out.
big tech wants to be buddies with fascism. They're increasingly working with the US Military and surveillance apparatus on things we won't hear about until its too late.
At the same time, they're spending billions to get you hooked on AI while providing those tools well below cost. Don't be fooled, they won't stay cheap forever.
They know it makes you dumber. They want you unable to function without it.
They will only be satisfied once they have captivated every single second of your life with algorithmically delivered content and convinced you to outsource all cognition.
That's on my mind a lot lately.

Anyways, I can't control my government or their thugs, but I can pull myself back from the corporate web.
Let's see how this goes.
